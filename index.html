Let's Start!

Rules:

1. Press "Roll Dice" to roll the dice. Each dice is added to the previous ones. You can see your current score in "current".

2. If the dice drops "1" your current score goes to zero and turn passes to the next player.

3. Press "Hold" if you want to store your current score. It will be added to the summary.

4. Score 100 points to win!
